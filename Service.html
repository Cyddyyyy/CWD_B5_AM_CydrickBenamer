Qualifications of a Franchisee

Enterprising with strong desire to succeed.
Must have a strong background in people skills.
Wiiling to undergo full-time training in restaurant operations.
Must be hands-on and willing to devote time overseeing day-to-day operations.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

How much Investment is needed and what does this cover?
The Franchise package ranges from P9 million to P12 million. This includes the franchise fee, store construction, equipment, furniture and fixtures, manpower hiring and training and pre-opening marketing activities. However, the investment cost varies based on floor area and locations, i.e., Provincial areas, Greater Manila Area, Metro Manila and others.
What’s the floor area requirement?

The required floor area ranges from 100sqm to 120sqm.

Can I apply for a franchise even if i don’t have a site or location?
Yes. Once qualified as a franchisee, we can offer available locations.
Can a corporation apply for a franchise?
Yes! But we require majority ownership that will act as Managing Director of the franchise
How much is the expected return of investment
There are several controllable and uncontrollable factors that will determine the ROI.
How long is the franchise term?
The franchise term for a Pancake House store is ten (10) years and renewable for another five (5) years.
Who looks for the location?
The company has a pipeline but we entertain in case the franchise applicant has a proposed or preferred location.
Is there a standard store design?
Yes, Fluffy Pony provides, through an accredited architect, the standard store design, layout, equipment and facilities requirement.
