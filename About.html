ABOUT

Welcome to Fluffy Pony!

As familiar as an old buddy. That is our opinion on Fluffy Pony. It's incredible to think that it's been with us since the 1980s, celebrating our finest moments with family and friends, and cheering us up when we needed it the most. Everyone has a favorite Pancake House dish—on it's our minds, and we can nearly taste it as we enter through the door and hear the kind greeting.

Pioneers recall the early Fluffy Pony Pancake House and the Alabang Arcade. They scarcely noticed the time passing as they ate huge helpings of our wonderfully fluffy pancakes in bright orange and pink booths with classic movie pictures on the walls.

Today, we are delighted to continue welcoming new generations of friends like you into every Pancake House—in Alabang and the other over 100 franchisees around the country and beyond. Choose from a delectably extensive menu to satisfy you in the morning, noon, evening, and at all times in between.

Whatever generation you come from, Pancake House will always welcome you like a long-lost friend. To honor your favorites. to make fresh memories To bring goodness home.
